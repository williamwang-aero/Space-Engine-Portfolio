#Planet 1 Hypothesis
For organisms to survive at this temp, it must have high atmospheric pressure. Since this is a gas giant, I suspect that the life forms must exist deep in the layer of this planet for water to exist. These lif forms must be very light as well, which means it must be nearly entirely made out of hydrogen in order for it to float. Furthermore, I suspect these multicellular organisms must be incredibly small in order to survive in this temp. I got this idea from hyperhtermophiles, which lives in the bottom of our ocean in extreme conditions. They survive using unique robust proteins, a monolayer, supercoiled DNA, an energy source, and a special product that puffs their internal cellular contents.
